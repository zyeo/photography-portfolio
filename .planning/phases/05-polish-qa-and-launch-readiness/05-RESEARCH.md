# Research — Phase 5

## Locked product decisions
- The site should be fast, stable, accessible, and reproducible to deploy.
- The website is not the sole archive of original files.
- Public pages should degrade gracefully when content is sparse or missing.

## Implementation strategy
1. Add machine-readable launch surfaces first: metadata, sitemap, robots, empty/not-found states.
2. Add operator-facing readiness docs and explicit image-delivery guidance.
3. Finish with a QA pass across routes, build output, and browser-visible behavior.
